## Idea
Saralo is an AI-powered browser accessibility platform that transforms complex websites into simple, personalized, and easy-to-navigate experiences. It is designed for elderly users, neurodivergent individuals (such as people with ADHD and dyslexia), and anyone who struggles to understand or navigate modern websites. As essential services like government portals, healthcare, banking, and education move online, complex interfaces often prevent these users from accessing information independently. Unlike traditional accessibility tools that only adjust fonts or colors, Saralo uses Generative AI to simplify webpage layouts, summarize content, streamline forms, and personalize the browsing experience based on individual needs. By reducing cognitive overload and improving digital accessibility, Saralo empowers millions of users to confidently access essential online services, promoting digital inclusion and ensuring technology is accessible to everyone
